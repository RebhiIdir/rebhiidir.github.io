## Objective

Build a desktop management application for an industrial oil mill, as the final-year project for a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, completed in a two-person team.

## Technical scope

- Desktop GUI built with Python, PyQt5, and Qt Designer.
- MySQL database for persisting operational data.
- Full UML modeling of the system prior to implementation.
- Written thesis documenting the design and development process.

## Results

A working desktop application covering the oil mill's core management workflows, delivered together with UML documentation and a final-year thesis report.

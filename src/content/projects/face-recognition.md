## Objective

Build a biometric facial recognition system: detect faces, extract discriminative features, train a recognition model, and evaluate it rigorously.

## Technical scope

- Face detection and image preprocessing.
- Feature extraction for face representation.
- Model training on labeled face data.
- Testing and performance evaluation phases.

## Results

The pipeline runs end to end from raw images to a trained recognition model, with a dedicated test and evaluation phase to measure performance — full details and metrics are in the linked report.

## Research connection

Direct precursor to the thesis work: both projects deal with extracting stable, meaningful signal (visual identity here, latent noise statistics there) from image data under a deep learning pipeline.

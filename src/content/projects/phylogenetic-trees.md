Implement the algorithms needed to construct phylogenetic trees from biological sequence data, applying graph theory and algorithmic rigor to a bioinformatics problem.

## Technical scope

- Distance-based tree construction from biological data.
- Graph-theoretic representation and traversal of the resulting tree structures.
- Visualization of the constructed phylogenetic trees.

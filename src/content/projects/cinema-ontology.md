## Objective

Model the domain of cinema — films, people, roles, genres, and their relationships — as a formal ontology suitable for semantic web integration.

## Technical scope

- Domain analysis and concept modeling for the cinema domain.
- Formal ontology definition (classes, properties, relationships).
- Design for interoperability with semantic web tooling and queries.

## Results

A structured, queryable ontology of the cinema domain, documented and available on GitHub.
